Fun Facts About Guinea Pigs:

  • Guinea pigs need to be introduced to a diverse diet early as pups. If you introduce foods that they have not eaten and they don't like it, they will starve themselves.
  • In some parts of Central and South America they are a delicacy (Peru, Ecuador, etc)
