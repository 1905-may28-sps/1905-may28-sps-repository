Pizza

One of New York City's most iconic food with a long history. Chef's have altered the recipe throughout the ages, but are their creations masterpieces or abominiations? Can they still be called pizza?

There are so many different types, I'll just give my opinion on a few listed below.
  • Margherita pizza
  • New York City Pizza
  • Desert Pizza
  • Cauliflower Pizza
  • Sushi pizza
  • Chicago Pizza

Margherita Pizza

One of the oldest styles of making pizza, named after Queen Consort Margerita.

The colors of the pizza was meant to represent the national colors of italy.

A slice of pizza that has last through the ages, how can it not be good!

NYC Pizza

Cauliflower Pizza

Thankfully, this is still not a pizza needs to have a dough crust!

I never truly understood the reasoning behind this pizza.

If it is because you have celiac diseases, ok thats fair, this is a decent subsitite.

But, if your having this because you believe it to be healthier? You might be suprised

There are many brands of cauliflower pizzas, and many of them are actually unhealthier then just having a standard wheat crust pizza.

Take away, unless you have some dietary restriction, at least do your taste buds a favor and have a real pizza slice.

Sushi Pizza

If you created this monstrosity, you either severely dissapointed your master or your not a legit sushi chef.

Pizza is more then just the shape, it is its ingredients and how it is made.
