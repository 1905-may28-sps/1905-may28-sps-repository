My Trip to Honduras

July - August 2018

Country of Destination. Honduras. My travel companions. Me, Myself, and I. Last July, 2018, i ventured forth to volunteer with the organization Students Helping Honduras. The non profit, founded by Japanese American and CNN heroe, Shin Fujiyama, alleviates the problem of poverty, restoring dignity amongst the people, especially the youth. Shin, was able to muster a group of 46 families who were living in a Shanti Town along a river to change their lives for the better. Together, they relocated to a swamp, developed the land, built a futbol field, 46 homes, a watchtower, bilingual school, and children's home in a short span of 10 years. Now he continues to help the youth of Honduras by building schools throughout the country. The goal is to build 1000 schools. The current count, over 50. I myself was fortunate to be a a part of 3.
