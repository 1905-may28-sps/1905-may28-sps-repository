Personal Life

Tiffany was born on May 20th, in Brooklyn, New York to Whitfield West Jr. and Deborah George-West. She has one older sister Whitney (West) Richardson. Her Mother was born in St. Thomas U.S.V.I. and moved to the states at the age of 10. Her father is a Brooklyn native. Tiffany attended Holy Rosary and P.S. 40 (George Washington Carver) for elementary school, I.S. 271 for middle and Science Skills Center for high school. For college she attended St. francis and Medgar Evers College where she majored in Computer Information Systems.
